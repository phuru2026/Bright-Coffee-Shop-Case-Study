# Bright-Coffee-Shop Background
The Bright Coffee Shop Sales Analysis case study focuses on analyzing daily transactional data to support business growth and performance improvement.  
- As a data analyst, my goal is to extract meaningful insights from historical sales data to understand customer behavior, identify high-performing products, and uncover trends across different time periods.
- These insights will guide strategic decisions and improve overall business performance and help the CEO to make data-driven business decisions.


### Project Description & Raw Data
This folder includes original files that explains what the assignment is about, and the data that was used. Everything is in its original format and nothing was changed.


Project's main focus:

1. Which products generate the most revenue?
2. When does the store perform best?
3. What are the sales patterns across time and products?
4. Where are the opportunities to improve performance?

### Data Processing
Databricks was used to ingest and process data. I used SQL query to process the data and read the script. In Databricks we get to sort and filter data, step by step until all data was combined into one big table.  
Key Performance Indicators:  
- 💰 Revenue
1. Total Revenue: calculated as unit_price × transaction_qty.
2. Revenue by Product Category: identifies which products contribute most to overall sales.
3. Revenue Trends Over Time: tracks performance across different days and time intervals

   
- 🛍️ Product Performance
1. Top-Selling Products: products with the highest revenue or quantity sold.
2. Low-Performing Products: items that generate low sales and may need promotion or removal.
3. Total Units Sold: measures demand for each product category


- Time Based
1. Peak Sales Periods: identifies high-traffic time intervals.
2. Off-Peak (Low Sales) Periods: reveals opportunities for targeted promotions.
3. Sales by Time of Day: compares performance across morning, afternoon, and evening.
4. Transaction Frequency by Time: number of purchases within each time interval


- 📈 Operational & Efficiency
1. Average Transaction Value (ATV)
2. Average spend per transaction
3. Sales Volume per Interval
4. Total transactions grouped by time buckets
5. Product Mix Efficiency
6. Balance between high-performing and low-performing products
